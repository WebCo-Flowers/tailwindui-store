The Symbolism and Meaning Behind Red Roses

Ever wonder why roses are everywhere, from classic paintings to expensive perfumes? It’s because roses are more than just a feast for the eyes ‒ they’ve coloured our world for thousands of years, symbolizing love, passion, and even a pinch of secrecy (shh!) Let’s look at some of the symbolism and meanings behind red roses that still stands to this day:
  • Love and Romance:With roots tracing back to ancient times, red roses are the most timeless way to say, “I love you.”
  • Beauty and Perfection: In its striking elegance, the red rose symbolizes beauty and perfection.
  • Courage and Respect: Red roses aren’t only about love, but also courage, respect, and admiration.
  • Mystery and Intrigue. Sometimes, love has layers and an air of mystery. Red roses have long been seen to hint at hidden depths or passion (spicy!)
  • Celebration and Congratulations.Graduations, promotions, anniversaries, you name it ‒ in life’s happiest moments, red roses are there to celebrate.

Red Roses: How to Keep Them Fresh

Your fresh-cut roses can look beautiful for longer with a little TLC! Follow these simple steps to get the most out of your dozen red roses:
  • Use a Clean Vase.:Give your favourite vase a good scrub with soap and water before you use it to remove pesky bacteria or residue.
  • Fill with Warm Water: Aim for room temperature, like a cozy bath for your roses.
  • Trim Stems: Chop off about an inch from the bottom, and remember, the angle is everything! A 45-degree cut makes your stems thirsty for water. Use sharp scissors or pruning shears so you don’t squish the stems.
  • Pluck Low Leaves: See any leaves below the water? Pluck ’em off. Leaves underwater are party spots for bacteria.
  • Remove Fading Flowers: If any roses start to look sad and droopy, it’s time to say goodbye. Removing them keeps the rest of the bunch happy and healthy.
Repeat These Steps Every 2 to 3 Days.Fresh water, a clean vase, and a quick trim – it’s like a mini spa day to keep your roses looking fresh and beautiful for longer!

What Does the Number of Roses Given Mean?

Ever wonder why a dozen roses feels so right? Twelve roses isn’t just a random number – it has been a symbol of complete and perfect love for ages.

One dozen red roses symbolizes a perfect, complete bond. It’s a full circle, a complete story. Buying a dozen red roses is like saying, “You’re my everything” twelve times over. It’s no wonder this bouquet is the perfect pick for anniversaries, Valentine’s Day, or just because!
