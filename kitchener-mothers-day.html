Same-Day Flower Delivery
in Kitchener

Life is busy — we get it. Gone are the days of rushing to grab a bouquet of half-wilted flowers at the grocery store on your way to a special occasion. At Forest of Flowers Kitchener, you can trust our florists to put together a beautiful, fresh and elegant flower arrangement and have it delivered to your doorstep the very same day.

While we can't guarantee an exact delivery time, we typically complete deliveries within four hours. Feel free to mention your preferred time slot in the special instructions, and we’ll give it our all to honour it. Our delivery services start at just $12 per order. Our Promise

  • Flowers Just Like the Photos Rest assured that the freshness and beauty you see in our photos is similar to what we deliver to your doorstep! The arrangement you order will be handcrafted by a local florist using fresh, local flowers so it’s everything you imagine and more.
  • Timely Delivery: We know that timing matters as much as the flowers themselves! Order at least a day before and our flowers will reach you exactly as promised, never late and not a moment too soon.
  • Guaranteed Quality: “Field Fresh Quality for Less” is more than a motto! We source our flowers as locally as possible so they stay fresh longer — brightening the room for days after arrival. We stand firmly behind the quality of our flowers and our service and proudly offer a 100% Unconditional Satisfaction Guarantee.
  • Outstanding Value:We pride ourselves on offering premium bouquets at fair and transparent prices. There are no hidden fees, even for last-minute orders. Plus, every order earns you Petal Perks rewards, saving you on every order after that!
  • Personal Touches: Want to add a message straight from the heart? You can! Choose specific arrangements or include a personal note, so your gift is as unique as the bond you share.
  • Handled with Care, Every Step of the WayOur flower delivery team treats every flower with the gentleness it deserves. From our doors to yours, we ensure your bouquet arrives in perfect condition — no wilts, no damage!
