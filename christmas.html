The Beauty of Flowers for Christmas

Whether you’re decking the halls or sending a sleigh-load of love across the miles, there's always a reason to light up someone’s season with holiday flowers!
  • Holiday Cheer at Home: Want to make your living room look like Santa’s workshop met a winter wonderland? A vibrant bouquet can do just that. It's like elf magic for your home decor!
  • A Yuletide Surprise for Loved Ones Far Away: Can’t make it over the river and through the woods to grandma’s house this year? Send a festive floral hug instead. It's the next best thing to being there in person!
  • Spreading Joy to Unexpected Places: From the helpful neighbor who always shovels your sidewalk to the teacher guiding your children through another adventurous year, a Christmas bouquet is a wonderful way to say, "thanks for being awesome".
  • Brightening the Holiday Table: Whether it’s the big Christmas dinner or just cozy hot cocoa nights, flowers add that special glow to every meal. It’s like dining under the stars (minus the chilly night air.)
  • Secret Santa and Gift Exchanges: Forget the generic gift card! Be the MVP of secret Santa with a gorgeous arrangement that says, “I put thought into this.”
  • Just Because: Sometimes, the best reason is no reason at all. Surprise someone (or treat yourself) just because it’s Christmas and flowers make everything better.

Our Most Popular Christmas Flowers

  • White Roses: Elegant and pure, white roses are a timeless classic for the holiday season. Their pristine beauty symbolizes peace and joy, making them a perfect addition to any Christmas flower arrangement.
  • Green Fuji Mums: Vibrant and eye-catching, green fuji mums add a pop of lively colour and unique texture to Christmas bouquets. Their lush, starburst-like blooms remind us of Christmas decorations, ringing in the joy and excitement of the season.
  • Evergreens: Can’t get a real Christmas tree this year? The rich, deep green hues and refreshing scent of our evergreen sprigs are another way to bring a piece of the outdoor winter wonderland inside.
  • White Oriental Lilies: With their large, fragrant blooms, white oriental lilies are a luxurious choice for the holidays. They add an enchanting touch to traditional holiday arrangements.
  • White Cremones: With intricate patterns similar to snowflakes, white cremones are fantastic for adding texture and volume to your holiday bouquets. Their fluffy, ruffled petals capture the essence of a snowy Christmas morning.
  • White Alstroemerias: Also known as Peruvian lilies, white alstroemerias symbolize friendship and devotion, making them a thoughtful addition to any Christmas arrangement.
  • White Carnations: Soft and fluffy, white carnations bring a touch of classic charm to holiday floral displays. Their long-lasting blooms and sweet fragrance make them a favourite for creating lasting Christmas memories.

Add an Extra Festive Touch to Your Christmas Arrangement

While a bouquet of flowers beautifully expresses your warmth and festive cheer during Christmas, sometimes, you want to add a little something extra. At Forest of Flowers, we offer a range of additional touches to enhance your floral gift and make every Christmas arrangement as unique as the holiday spirit itself

  • Balloons A balloon, especially one with a "Merry Christmas" message, adds a festive touch and turns the gift into a mini-celebration.
  • Teddy Bears Teddy bears are there to give you a warm embrace whenever you need it! It's a thoughtful choice if you can’t be there to share the holiday cheer in person.
  • Christmas Cards Sometimes, there's so much joy and holiday wishes, and a bouquet alone might not capture it all. A card provides the space to pen down heartfelt emotions, festive greetings, or even a simple note of appreciation.

Every gesture, whether big or small, plays a role in crafting a memorable Christmas surprise. With these thoughtful additions, you can transform a beautiful floral arrangement into an unforgettable holiday experience!
